Architecture

How Tauron Works

Step 1 Ingestion

CSV upload, voice entry, or REST API. Standardized sensor streams per cow.

Step 2 Graph Build

Pen assignments + feeding logs → PyG contact graph. Edges encode disease transmission probability.

Step 3 GNN Inference

GraphSAGE + GRU over 7-day windows. Outputs mastitis / BRD / lameness risk per node.

Step 4 LLM Alert

Gradient XAI extracts evidence. Ollama → Claude → template generates farmer-readable directive.

No proximity sensors needed. The pipeline synthesizes everyday barn layout and feeding logs to infer contact networks without expensive hardware.
The Key Innovation When Cow A's health degrades, her neighbors' risk scores update through message passing — even if they look fine right now.
