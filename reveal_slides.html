Architecture

How Tauron Works

1. Ingestion

Standardize raw CSV & API sensor streams.

2. Graph Modeling

Translate physical behaviors (shared pens & bunks) into mathematical networks.

3. GNN Inference

GraphSAGE+GRU spatio-temporal layers monitor 7-day windows for 48h early warnings.

4. Alerting

Extract critical evidence and dispatch the LLM warning.

No proximity sensors needed. The pipeline synthesizes everyday barn layout and feeding logs to infer accurate contact networks without expensive hardware.

One Multi-Head Model. Trained globally on Wageningen SensOor profiles to detect distinct disease vectors accurately from a single baseline graph.
