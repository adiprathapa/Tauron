  • Masked symptoms until damage is done
  • Prolonged treatment risks permanent lung damage
  • Permanent production drops

Lameness $300–$500 per case
  • Chronically under-diagnosed by eye
  • Hidden cost through milk loss
  • Reproductive failure & early culling
